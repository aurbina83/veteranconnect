HOW IT WORKS

Browse

Browse events created by other veterans based on your location. Each event will tell you how many people are invited, the branch of service of the event creator, and the location. Search from 5 to 50 mile radius.

Create

Create your events and meet local veterans interested in what you want to do. Whether you're looking to organize a large event, or gather a small group to hang out, VETERAN CONNECT is your platform.

Attend

Found an event you're interested in? If there are open slots left, join in and chat with other veterans attending. Back out of the event if you're no longer interested, opening the spot for another veteran.
