HOW IT WORKS

Browse

Browse events created by other veterans based on your location. Each event will tell you how many people are invited, the branch of service of the event creator, and the location. Search from 5 to 50 mile radius.

Create

Create your events and meet local veterans interested in what you want to do. Whether you're looking to organize a large event, or gather a small group to hang out, VETERAN CONNECT is your platform.

Attend

Found an event you're interested in? If there are open slots left, join in and chat with other veterans attending. Back out of the event if you're no longer interested, opening the spot for another veteran.

SET THE RADIUS

Slide the radius tool to select a search radius from 5 to 50 miles.

Event Card

Each event card contains information about the event. Click more info for full address and to see who else is going.

Go

Click go to attend the event. You'll be able to find and manage your events in the app menu.

Where to Meet?

Choose the rally point where either the event is taking place, or where everybody will meet.

You can search for actual places like 'The Oakland Bar', or terms like "food, rock climbing, parks".

Create the Event

Decide on what to do, and set the number of people you want to show up.

Manage your Events

On your events page, you can edit or cancel your events at any time.

Can't make it an event you're attending? Back out of it by hitting the "X" button. This opens up your spot for another veteran. Sort of like VA appointments. Oh how I love those.

Click any of these events to go the event page.

Interact

On the event page, you'll see all available information for the event.

Get to know the other veterans in your area that are attending in the comments section.
